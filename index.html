Industrial Cadre for Operation & Maintenance(ICOM)

ICOM is an ISO 9001-2008 Certified company which is specialized in the multiple aspects of Industrial Operation & Maintenance Services. ICOM offers its safe & quality services to Oil & Gas, Petro-chemical, Power & Energy, Fertilizers and Metal Plants. Our establishment is strategically located in Jubail Industrial 2 Saudi Arabia. Our facility is located on 50,000 sqm area which is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment & latest technology.
